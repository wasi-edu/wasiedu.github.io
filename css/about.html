About Study Hall

Study Hall is a small, free library of study notes, written to help students revise faster and understand concepts more clearly. Everything here is original — written by hand, chapter by chapter, subject by subject.

Why this exists

Good notes shouldn't sit behind a paywall or a sign-up form. This site exists to share clear, exam-focused notes with anyone who needs them, for free, with no account required.

What you'll find here

Notes are organized by subject and released as downloadable PDFs. New material is added regularly as it's written and reviewed.

A note on originality

All notes published on this site are original work. We do not upload scanned textbooks or copyrighted material — everything here is written specifically for this site.

Get in touch

Have a suggestion, spotted an error, or want to contribute your own notes? Visit the Contact page.
